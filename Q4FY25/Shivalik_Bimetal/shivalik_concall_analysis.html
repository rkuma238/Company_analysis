Key Topics Discussed:

  • Forward integration with smart DC sensor mounted shunts
  • New inductor applications in advanced development
  • BIS certification progress for domestic market protection
  • Recovery signs in bimetal segment from Q4
  • Strong growth in shunt resistor segment (30%+ in India)

Management Tone: Cautiously optimistic with confidence in double-digit growth for FY26. Strong focus on operational efficiency and strategic initiatives despite challenging market conditions.

2. Detailed Analysis

Business Model Evolution

Forward Integration Strategy: Major shift towards value-added products with smart DC current sensor assemblies. Management expects this to provide 10x value multiplication (from ₹10 shunt to ₹100+ assembly) with 40-50% EBITDA margins vs current 22-23%.

Revenue Impact: New PCBA segment expected to contribute ₹150 crores annually by FY27, representing significant revenue diversification.

Cost Structure: Bill of materials for new products around 15-16%, leaving substantial margin for value addition. Automation investments of ₹10-15 crores annually improving efficiency.

Industry Operating Environment

Key Industry Trends:
  • Smart metering acceleration driving shunt demand
  • EV adoption creating new application opportunities
  • China+1 strategy benefiting Indian manufacturers
  • Infrastructure upgrades globally increasing switchgear demand
  • Energy storage applications emerging as new growth driver

Management Tone and Sentiment

Management displayed measured confidence despite recent challenges. Key themes:

  • Realistic Optimism: Acknowledged past two years of stagnation but confident about recovery
  • Strategic Focus: Clear roadmap for value addition and market expansion
  • Operational Excellence: Emphasis on automation and efficiency improvements
  • Customer Engagement: Strong visibility from customer discussions about 2x growth by 2028-29

4. Forward-Looking Statements and Guidance

Revenue Expectations

  • FY26 Growth: 15-18% overall growth targeted
  • Segment Breakdown: Shunt 15-18%, Bimetal 12-16%
  • Smart Meter Growth: 50% growth expected in FY26
  • Export-Domestic Mix: 55-58% exports, 44-47% domestic

Margin Expectations

  • Current EBITDA: Maintaining 22-23% levels
  • Forward Integration Impact: 40-50% EBITDA margins on new products
  • Scale Benefits: 20% volume scale-up expected to improve margins

Growth Drivers

  • Smart Metering: Currently 10% of revenue, growing 50% annually
  • Automotive/EV: 37% of revenue, seeing renewed traction in Q4
  • Energy Storage: Small but emerging segment with significant potential
  • BIS Implementation: Expected to protect domestic market from Chinese imports
  • Geographic Expansion: Europe and ASEAN showing strong growth

5. Risk Assessment

Competitive Threats

  • Chinese Competition: Aggressive pricing pressure, though BIS may provide relief
  • Technology Disruption: Need continuous R&D investment to stay competitive
  • Customer Concentration: Dependence on key automotive and industrial customers

Execution Risks

  • Forward Integration Timeline: 12-18 month validation cycles for new products
  • Capex Requirements: Significant investment needed for new production lines
  • Market Adoption: Customer acceptance of higher-value integrated products

Market-Specific Risks

  • US-China Trade Tensions: Potential impact on global supply chains
  • EV Market Volatility: Dependence on automotive electrification pace
  • Regulatory Changes: BIS implementation timeline uncertainty
